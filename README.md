# TrustID — Karnataka UBID & Business Intelligence Prototype

TrustID is an **integrity-first Unified Business Identifier (UBID) and Active Business Intelligence prototype** built for Karnataka Commerce & Industry.

It helps government officers resolve fragmented business records across multiple departments into a single trusted business identity, classify whether a business is active/dormant/closed, identify inspection gaps, flag suspicious identity fragmentation, and maintain a tamper-evident decision ledger.

---

## Problem Statement

Government departments often maintain separate business records in different formats and systems.

A single real-world business may appear differently across systems such as:

- Shop Establishment
- Factories
- Labour
- KSPCB
- BESCOM
- BWSSB
- Food Safety

Sri Lakshmi Precision Tools Pvt Ltd
S L Precision Tools Private Limited
